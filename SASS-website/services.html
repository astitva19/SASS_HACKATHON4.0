Impact on Farmer’s Life

With the use of this technology, the farmer no longer requires to be around his land day in and day out. We are providing a revolution to the agricultural sector leading to – Internet of Farming. Thus it would make farmers mobile and they can control their farms from anywhere in the world. Apart from this, they will be guided about best practices related to farming for their own profit based on soil, weather analysis. The bot would upload the amount of crop harvested on the portal where prices will be regulated via govt. and thus eradicate the requirement of middlemen.

Impact on other businesses :

As all the farmers would be registered to our services it will serve as a ripe platform for advertisements related to agriculture business, banks, insurance, telecom industry, IT personnels etc. Thus it will open a plethora of jobs and oppurtunities with it & it will also support our prime minister’s vision of digital india.

Impact on Country :

Currently, agriculture sector contributes about 17.32% to India’s GDP. With implementation of our project it will rise subsequently to a greater amount and increase our GDP. There would be reduced migration of people towards urban areas as increased oppurtunities related to every sector would be opened in villages itself. There would be human resource development also with this.
